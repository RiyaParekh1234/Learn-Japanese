ABOUT US!

Study Go Work JAPAN is an interview fair held in Tokyo during February, June, August, October and December every year. Participants will be invited to Tokyo for interviews with leading Japanese companies. A permanent job placing will be offered when successful.

We invite approximately 35-55 students all over Asia for each round of Study Go Work JAPAN.


THE PROCESS
Submit your application here. We will review your application on a rolling basis. If your profile meets our requirements, we will have a Skype Interview with you. The purpose of the Skype Interview is for us to get to know you better. It's a casual interview and will take approximately 10-15 minutes.
Successful candidates who have completed the Skype Interview will be asked to submit a 1-minute PR video of themselves. Your resume and PR video will be submitted to the participating companies.
About 3 weeks before the interview event, we will inform you of the results: Successful candidates will be invited to Japan to take part in the interview event.
